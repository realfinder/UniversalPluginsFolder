SpotLight

SpotLight function illuminates a specified circular disc area. The illumination color can be specified and remains constant. The spot center moves from the initial center coordinates in pixels to the final coordinates. Dim controls the factor by which the scene is dimmed prior. RGB, YUY2 and Planar 8/9-16/32 bit depth formats are supported.
